# Submitting Audio Clips

Thanks for helping with the Ani Voice Rebuild project.

## What to submit

Useful clips usually have:

- one speaker only
- clear speech
- minimal background noise
- minimal overlap with music or sound effects
- target length of 3–15 seconds per clip

Accepted formats:

- .wav
- .mp3
- .m4a
- .mp4

Raw recordings are welcome. Clean segmented clips are also welcome.

## What to avoid

Please avoid submitting:

- private recordings you are not comfortable sharing
- multiple speakers in the same clip
- heavy background music
- distorted audio
- clips shorter than 2 seconds
- very long recordings without labeling

## Naming

If uploading segmented clips, simple names are fine:

- ani_raw_001.wav
- ani_raw_002.wav
- ani_raw_003.wav

Files will be normalized later during preprocessing.

## Privacy

Please only submit audio you are comfortable contributing to an open collaborative project.
